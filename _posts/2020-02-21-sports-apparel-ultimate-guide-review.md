## Care and Maintenance Tips

### Retro Football Shirts
- Inside-out washing on gentle cycle
- Air dry to preserve fabric and prints
- Store properly to maintain shape
- Avoid harsh detergents

### Performance Tights
- Wash after each use to prevent odor
- Use cold water to maintain elasticity
- Avoid fabric softeners (reduces wicking)
- Air dry to prevent heat damage

> **Pro Tip:** Rotate between multiple pairs to extend lifespan - continuous wear breaks down elastic fibers faster!
